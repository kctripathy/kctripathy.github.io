Welcome to My Website

Hi! I’m Kishor Chandra Tripathy, the mind behind this space.

I am a passionate software developer having more than two decades of experience and a curious individual with a deep love for technology, creativity, classical music, plantation and sports.

I like to live a simple life and believe in making a positive impact with everything I do. Whether it's through the melodies of the Indian Bamboo flute, harmonium lines of code, sharing knowledge, etc...

About me

Born on the culturally rich lands of Odisha, India, I’ve grown up at Tumudibanda and at my village ( Bijaya Dhanurjayapur - Sasan ), surrounded by a deep appreciation for plantation, folk-art, tradition, and the power of technology to transform lives.

Over the years, I’ve cultivated a unique path that merges two passions — software development and the soulful melodies of the Indian classical music using bamboo flute (bansuri) and harmonium.
