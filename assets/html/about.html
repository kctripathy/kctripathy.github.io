About me

I am software developer by profession and a musician at heart. I work with modern web technologies like Angular and .NET, and also create flute-based Indian classical music content on my YouTube Channel Channel.

On 10th August 1975 (Srabana Sukla Chaturthi), I born from Late Sri Ramakant Tripathy and Smt. Subasini Tripathy, in the village Lunipada. I have two brothers and four sisters.

My father was a Kabiraj (Ayurvedic Doctor) and also working as a state government employee. So, we used to roam with his place of employment. So, till class five I was studying in Tumudibanda, Khandhamal and on 1984 we shifted permanently to our native village BD Pur (sasan).

My native village, Bijaya Dhanurjayapur - Sasan, Ganjam (Odisha) is surrounded by hill, green thus a deep inspiration for plantation, folk-art, tradition, and the power of technology to transform lives.

Over the years, I’ve cultivated a unique path that merges two passions — software development and the soulful melodies of the Indian classical music using bamboo flute (bansuri) and harmonium.

I like to live a simple life and believe in making a positive impact with everything I do. Whether it's through the lines of Code, the melodies of the Indian Bamboo Flute, Harmonium, Keyboard (CASIO), sharing knowledge, etc...
