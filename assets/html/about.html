About me

Once in a blue moon day, on 10th August 1975 (Srabana Sukla Chaturthi), I born from Late Sri Ramakant Tripathy and Smt. Subasini Tripathy, in the village Lunipada. I have two brothers and four sisters. Currently living in Bangalore with my wife and a son.

My father was a Kabiraj (Ayurvedic Doctor) and also working as a state government employee. So, we used to roam with his place of employment. So, till class five I was studying in Tumudibanda, Khandhamal and on 1984 we shifted permanently to our native village BD Pur (sasan).

My village, Bijaya Dhanurjayapur - Sasan, Ganjam (Odisha) is surrounded by hill, green thus a deep inspiration for plantation, folk-art, tradition, and the power of technology to transform lives.

Over the years, I’ve cultivated a unique path that merges two passions — software development and the soulful melodies of the Indian classical music using bamboo flute (bansuri) and harmonium.
